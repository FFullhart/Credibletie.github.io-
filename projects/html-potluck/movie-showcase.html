 Ponyo

  GENRE: Animation/Fantacy/Family Film    RUNTIME: 1hr 43min    RATING:

 Overview

Ponyo is a 2008 Studio Ghibli animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki, focusing on a magical goldfish princess who longs to be come human after befriending a 5-year-old boy named Sosuke. The story follows their heartwarming, innocent friendship as Ponyo escapes her wizard father, uses magic to gain human form, and unintentionally causes a massive, dangerous imbalance in the world.
