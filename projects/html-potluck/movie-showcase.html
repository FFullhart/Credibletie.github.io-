 Ponyo

  GENRE: Animation/Fantacy/Family Film    RUNTIME: 1hr 43min    RATING:

 Overview

Ponyo is a 2008 Studio Ghibli animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki, focusing on a magical goldfish princess who longs to become human after befriending a 5-year-old boy named Sosuke. The story follows their heartwarming, innocent friendship as Ponyo escapes her wizard father, uses magic to gain human form, and unintentionally causes a massive, dangerous imbalance in the world.

 Totoro

  GENRE: Animation/Fantacy/Slice-of-Life     RUNTIME: 1hr 28min    RATING:

 Overview

This acclaimed animated tale by director Hayao Miyazaki follows schoolgirl Satsuke and her younger sister, Mei, as they settle into an old country house with their father and wait for their mother to recover from an illness in an area hospital. As the sisters explore their new home, they encounter and befriend playful spirits in their house and the nearby forest, most notably the massive cuddly creature known as Totoro.
